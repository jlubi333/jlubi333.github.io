About Me

Hello, I am Justin Lubin! I love math, computer science, and playing the piano. As of now, I am a junior in high school. I've loved proramming ever since I was in sixth grade. One of my favorite websites is Project Euler. Check it out!

My favorite programming languages are Haskell, Python, and C. Haskell has a brutal purity that awes/scares me; the source code of a Haskell program looks like a work of art. I also really like Python because it is super easy to prototype code and I believe that the language is designed very nicely. Additionally, I've also looked at Clojure a little bit and it seems nice.

Playing the piano is another passion of mine. I started in eighth grade when I was thirteen, so that means I've been playing for about four years. My favorite composers are Chopin, J.S. Bach, and Rachmaninoff.

Note that this site's source code is available at Bitbucket free of charge. It's completely open source, so take whatever you want!
